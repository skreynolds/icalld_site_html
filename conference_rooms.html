14 - 16 February 2018 | Charles Darwin University

ICALLD Symposium

The International Consortium of Academic Language and Learning Developers (ICALLD) Online Symposium 2018 was hosted by CDU from 14 - 16 February 2018.

There are links to the session recordings set up: one for the 14th of February, one for the 15th of February, and one for the 16th of February. The online conference rooms for the 14th, 15th and 16th can be accessed by clicking the Session Recording 1, Session Recording 2, or Session Recording 3 icons, respectively.


Abstracts and scheduled times can be found on the abstracts page located in the navigation menu above. Similarly, instructions on how to use the Blackboard Collaborate interface, where the symposium will be hosted, can be found in the help page located in the navigation menu. Alternatively, a full schedule and abstract listing can be found in the ICALLD program provided in the link below.
